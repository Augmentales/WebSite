About Us

Augmented Reality (AR) and card games are coming together to create new experiences that mix physical and digital play.

ARena Duels, a modern card game app, aims to change the way players experience gaming by blending the real and virtual worlds. Using image processing and augmented reality tech, the game creates a fun battlefield where cards turn into interactive 3D characters.

Development Highlights

  • Image Processing Module: Recognizes and processes physical cards, ensuring correct placement in the game world.
  • Mobile AR Module: Creates the augmented reality interface, letting players see a lively overlay of physical cards and their digital versions.
  • Desktop Game Module: Handles game mechanics, turning player actions into visual and strategic moves on the virtual battlefield.
  • Communication Module: Ensures all game data is synced across modules, keeping the gameplay smooth.
